A brief description of the movie Grandma's Boy is a 2006 American stoner comedy. The main character is named Alex who is a 35 year old video game tester. After being evicted from his home, he moves in with his Grandma and her two roommates. He lies to his friends and coworkers telling them that he's living with hot babes.
